NowPlaying - HypeMachine

This chrome extension can tell Last.fm what you are currently listening to on HypeMachine.

It will not cause tracks to appear in your Last.fm track history, for that just enable the scrobbling feature offered by HypeMachine.

Instead, the track will appear as 'nowplaying', alowing other applications like NowPlaying to retrieve this data.
